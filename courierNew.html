courier new

To express Courier New’s aged psychedelic rock music I wanted to recreate film photographs from the 40s and 50s as best as possible, with a somber touch as per the artist’s request. This I did by dampening the saturation heavily, increasing the black levels, adding grain, and attempting to portray the subject as if they were the only individual left on earth.

Photoshoot - June 19 2020 - Photographs done by me - edited in Adobe Lightroom- Styling done by Courier New
