about

Henry Abrams is a second-year student studying Art and Design at Northeastern University in Boston Massachusetts. He is relatively new to the field, only finding his interest in album cover and movie poster design in the Summer of 2020, but he has fallen in love with the craft. Using various techniques and an excess of inspirations that range from the Japanese ukiyo-e style of woodblock prints and painting to comic book design, Abrams uses creation as a medium to step away and consider. His favorite artists include names such as Cassandre, Paula Scher, and S. Neil Fujita.
